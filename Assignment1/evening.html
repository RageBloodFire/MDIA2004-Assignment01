This is where things get interesting!
I left campus on the shcool bus at 8pm and arrived at the bus park with my friends to get another bus to go home.
We didn't see any of our regular busses, but we saw an old, delapidated HIACE bus.
It had only 2 windows and we really didn't want to take it.
But we wanted to get home, so we got in.

About 10 minutes after leaving Mobay, the driver stopped at a gas station to get diesel.
He was reversing towards the pump and I swore I heard him hit a cat or something.
Anyway, he filled the tank and we left.
There was a stronger than normal fuel smell inside the vehicle, but we'll get back to that.

5-10 minutes after that, we reached the Great River check-point.
The driver slowed down so the soldiers could check the vehicle, then he proceeded...
We got about 5 minutes away from the check-point, then suddenly...

The Bus Broke Down!


Thick black smoke was coming from the front of the bus!
The smell of fuel became stronger!
The ONE door on the bus would not open!
EVERYONE BEGAN TO PANIC!

I jumped up and opened the window that was beside me!
I slipped through the window with such Grace and Agility!
Then I hurried to open the back to help my friends and the others out.
Once we were all out, we started walking briskly away from the bus because it was still smoking heavily.

Soon after, we got another bus and went home.
